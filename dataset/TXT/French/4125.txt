2gUbgbdJ7IFSbicBXlSchw After eating at many Montreal restaurants, this became my favorite.  The 'Maison du Ravioli' was spectacular, almost melting in the mouth.  The ravioli are filled with 3 kinds of meats and served in a veal/mushroom reduction.  The menu is the same for lunch and dinner.  

As in many Montreal restaurants, most dress nicely for dinner, but they will allow people with t-shirts in.  

If you are in Montreal, go here.  If you are not in Montreal, get yourself to Montreal and eat here.
